Beauty Contest

Difficulty Level: King of Diamonds

How to Play:

- Guess a number closest to 80% of everyone's average guess.

- All players: Same guesses = lose 1 point; no guess = lose 1 point.

- 2 players: One guesses 0 = lose 1 point, non-zero player wins; guess rounded target = winner, other loses 2 points; closest wins, other loses 1 point.

- 3 players: Guess rounded target = winner, others lose 2 points; closest wins, others lose 1 point.

- 4 or more players: Closest to target wins, others lose 1 point.
